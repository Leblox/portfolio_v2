Who am I?

Originally from Sweden, I have spent over a decade managing small, luxury boutique hotels in sunny London before turning towards coding and web development.

I have always actively saught out and introduced new technology to help improve profitability and efficiency in the properties I managed. I have always been amazed at the elegant problem solving that a good technology can provide. But after many years as the "client", I have also often been frustrated by poor design and the seemingly illogical "road-maps" of my favourite technology partners.

I love drinking tea from fancy cups and single-origin dark chocolate.

TL;DR Swedish, used to manage luxury hotels, now practice the dark arts of web development.
