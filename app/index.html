Who am I?

I am a design minded front-end developer focused on building beautiful websites, interfaces and experiences, supporting them with clean, elegant code. I am originally from the wilderness of northern Sweden, but have lived in London since 2007 and now know how to make a proper cup of tea.

I enjoy creating beautiful, functional designs working mainly with HTML5, SASS/CSS3 and JavaScript. I focus on keeping my code clean and efficient, and I make it a habit to stay up-to-date on current best practices as I work towards my goal of becoming a full-stack developer.

Before becoming a developer, I studied for a degree in Marketing & Business and have worked in the London luxury hotel industry for over a decade as a general manager in small luxury boutique hotels. This means that on top of five star people and communication skills, I am also great at getting things done and making good decisions under pressure.

In my spare time I enjoy reading, petting dogs I meet in the park and adding more words to my unpublished book.
